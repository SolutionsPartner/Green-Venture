OUR STORY

From Vision to
Sustainable Impact

Founded with a vision to democratize clean energy, Green Venture has grown from a passionate startup into Jharkhand's trusted solar energy partner. Based in Ranchi, we serve residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the region.

Led by Kavita Sinha, our team of dedicated engineers and energy consultants brings deep expertise and innovation to every project. We don't just install solar panels; we design intelligent energy systems tailored to each client's unique needs.

From off-grid power plants in remote villages to sophisticated rooftop installations for manufacturing facilities, our portfolio reflects our commitment to quality, reliability, and sustainable impact.
